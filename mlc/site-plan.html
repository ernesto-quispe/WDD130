Overview

Purpose

The purpose of our website is to be able to disseminate information about the municipality of Las Condes and the events that happen in the city. We want to keep all the inhabitants of Las Condes informed and more involved.

Audience

Our public will be men and women who live in Las Condes, they will be looking for information about the workings of the municipality like cleaning and vaccination schedules. They will also want information about sporting events, musical events, etc. Since it is a diverse audience, they will normally access our website through a laptop or smartphone.
